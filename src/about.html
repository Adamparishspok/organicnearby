About us

Welcome to OrganicNearby,
your one-stop-shop for discovering fresh, local, and organic food products. As a community focused search engine, OrganicNearby creates a Food-Hub in any community. It connects consumers with local farmers and producers, while promoting transparency within the consumers chosen travel distance.

OrganicNearby is unique in that we host multiple food hubs in one place, making it easy to find fresh, local, and organic food products in your community. Our platform connects shoppers with farmers, producers, and retailers, making it easy to find local food products sold directly to consumers. You can search for farms or brand by name or by the type of products you want to buy. By shopping with OrganicNearby, you are not only getting fresh, locally-grown organic food products but also supporting local businesses and contributing to the development of a local circular economy.
